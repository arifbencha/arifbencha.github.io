Why NVIC Matters

A Growing Community Deserves a Permanent Home

The Northern Valley of New Jersey — encompassing Closter, Demarest, Norwood, Harrington Park, Woodcliff Lake, and surrounding towns — is home to a thriving and rapidly growing Muslim community. Yet, there is no dedicated Islamic center to call our own.

We are in the process of acquiring a property — a former church at 283 Harrington Ave in Closter — that will be lovingly converted into our masjid. This transformation symbolizes the beauty of community, faith, and new beginnings. But we cannot do it without your support.

Every patron's donation — large or small — brings us one step closer to breaking ground and opening our doors to the community.

Community Impact

Stronger Together — For Everyone

NVIC is not just a mosque — it is a civic institution committed to enriching the entire Northern Valley community, regardless of faith or background.

🤝

Interfaith Dialogue

Regular sessions bringing together Muslim, Christian, Jewish, and other faith communities to foster mutual understanding, friendship, and peaceful coexistence.

🌍

Celebrating Diversity

Annual multicultural festivals, cultural showcases, and open-house events highlighting the rich tapestry of backgrounds within our community.

📚

Islamic Education

Weekend Islamic school, Quran classes, and adult education programs providing grounded religious knowledge and strong moral character.

🏥

Health & Wellness

Free health screenings, mental health support, and wellness workshops open to the entire community — because caring for the body is a spiritual act.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family & Youth Programs

Youth leadership programs, marriage counseling, parenting workshops, and after-school activities supporting families through every stage of life.

🕊️

Civic Engagement

Voter registration drives, town hall collaborations, and community events — ensuring Muslim voices contribute positively to our shared civic life.

What We Will Offer

Planned Community Services

01

Daily & Jumu'ah Prayers

Five daily prayers and congregational Friday Jumu'ah services with separate prayer spaces for men and women.

02

Food Pantry & Relief

Weekly food distribution open to all community members in need, with expanded Ramadan iftar meals served nightly.

03

Funeral Services

Islamic funeral arrangements, ghusl facilities, and bereavement support to help families navigate loss with dignity.

04

Nikah Ceremonies

Islamic marriage ceremonies with event space for receptions and family celebrations.

05

New Muslim Support

Dedicated programs for those embracing Islam — mentorship, learning resources, and a welcoming community network.

06

Counseling & Mediation

Confidential pastoral counseling for individuals, couples, and families, grounded in Islamic values.

07

Eid Celebrations

Large-scale Eid al-Fitr and Eid al-Adha prayers and community festivities open to all.

08

Community Hall

Multi-purpose event space for community organizations, non-profits, and family gatherings.
